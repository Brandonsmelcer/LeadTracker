Lead Tracker Privacy Policy

Internal sales & lead management for authorized team members

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Introduction

Vision To Legacy Group (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the Lead Tracker mobile and web application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices available to you.

The App is intended for authorized employees, managers, and administrators of Vision To Legacy Group and is not offered as a public consumer service. By creating an account or using the App, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide and data generated through your use of the App:

  • Account information: name, email address, role (admin, manager, or associate), and manager assignment where applicable.
  • Authentication credentials: email and password processed through Firebase Authentication. Passwords are hashed and not stored in plain text by us.
  • Business & sales data: county lead assignments, lead counts, sales dispositions (sold, undecided, not interested), sale amounts, follow-up or closing notes, and team communications you enter in the App.
  • Usage data: basic diagnostic and crash information that may be collected by our hosting providers to maintain service reliability.

We do not collect precise location, contacts from your device, photos, camera data, microphone recordings, health data, or advertising identifiers for cross-app tracking.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Authenticate users and enforce role-based access (admin, manager, associate).
  • Track, assign, and report on sales leads across Tennessee, Kentucky, and West Virginia counties.
  • Record sales outcomes, revenue, and follow-up notes for internal reporting.
  • Enable team messaging and operational communications within the App.
  • Maintain, secure, and improve the App and our internal business operations.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of use.

How We Store & Protect Data

Account profiles, lead records, and related business data are stored in Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore), hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure in the United States. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS/HTTPS).

Access within the App is restricted by user role. Only authorized Vision To Legacy personnel with valid credentials can sign in. Administrative features (such as map editing and CSV import) are limited to designated roles.

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only:

  • Within your organization: managers and administrators may view team lead and performance data according to their assigned role.
  • With service providers: Google Firebase / Google Cloud Platform, which process data on our behalf under their terms and privacy policies.
  • For legal reasons: when required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice where required by law.

Firebase privacy information: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Data Retention

We retain account and business data for as long as your organization uses the App and as needed for legitimate business, legal, or accounting purposes. When an account is deactivated or you leave the organization, we may retain or delete data according to our internal retention schedule or applicable law.

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or update your profile information through an administrator.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal and business retention requirements.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent (where applicable).

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe and as required by applicable law.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

International Users

The App is operated from the United States. If you access the App from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version on this page and update the “Last updated” date. Material changes may also be communicated through the App or by email where appropriate.
